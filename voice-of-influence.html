Voice of Influence Award (Podcast & Publishing)

Celebrating women who are shaping conversations, changing minds, and building audiences through the power of podcasting and publishing.

Enter this Category Download Questions

She Found Her Voice. And the World Leaned In.

Words have power — and some women know exactly how to use them. This award is for the women who've stepped up to the mic or put pen to paper and built something that matters. A podcast that people can't stop sharing. A book that changed how someone thinks. A platform that gives voice to stories that need to be heard.

Whether you host a podcast, authored a book, publish a newsletter, write a column, or create content that educates, inspires, and moves people — if your words have built an audience and made an impact, this is your award.

You're a great fit if you:

  • Host a podcast, authored a book, or publish content with a growing, engaged audience

  • Create content that educates, empowers, or inspires your listeners or readers

  • Have built a recognisable voice and platform in your niche or industry

  • Use your platform to uplift others, spark conversations, or drive meaningful change
