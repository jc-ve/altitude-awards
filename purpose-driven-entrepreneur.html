Purpose-Driven Entrepreneur Award

Celebrating women who built their business around something bigger than profit — driven by a mission that makes the world better.

Enter this Category Download Questions

She Didn't Start a Business to Get Rich. She Started It to Make a Difference.

For some women, business was never just about the bottom line. It was about a cause, a calling, a deep belief that things could be better — and the determination to build something that proves it. This award is for the women whose "why" is bigger than themselves. The ones who wake up every morning knowing that their work matters because it serves something greater.

Whether your purpose is environmental, social, cultural, health-related, or community-driven — if your business exists because you believe in something deeply and you've built a model that turns that belief into action, this is your award.

You're a great fit if you:

  • Built a business driven by a clear social, environmental, or community mission

  • Can demonstrate how your purpose directly shapes your business decisions and operations

  • Balance profit with impact — running a sustainable business that also serves a greater good

  • Inspire others through the authenticity and depth of your commitment to your cause
