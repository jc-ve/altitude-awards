She Doesn't Just Talk About Wellness. She Lives It and Leads It.

Wellness isn't a trend for you — it's a calling. This award is for the women who've built businesses around helping others feel better, move better, think better, and live better. The ones who show up every day with genuine care, deep knowledge, and an unwavering commitment to their clients' wellbeing.

Whether you work in fitness, nutrition, mental health, holistic therapies, skincare, or any corner of the wellness world — if your business exists because you believe everyone deserves to feel good in their own body and mind, this is your award.

You're a great fit if you:

  • Run a business in health, wellness, fitness, or wellbeing

  • Deliver real, lasting results that improve your clients' quality of life

  • Lead with integrity, evidence, and genuine care for people's health

  • Are passionate about making wellness accessible, inclusive, and empowering
