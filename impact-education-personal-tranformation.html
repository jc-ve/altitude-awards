Impact Award — Education & Personal Transformation

Celebrating women who empower others to learn, grow, and transform — unlocking potential through education, training, and personal development.

Enter this Category Download Questions

She Doesn't Just Teach. She Transforms Who People Believe They Can Be.

Knowledge changes everything — but the right teacher changes lives. This award is for the women who've dedicated their business to helping others learn, grow, and become someone they never thought they could be. The ones who don't just deliver information — they ignite confidence, shift mindsets, and unlock potential that was always there but never had permission to come out.

Whether you run workshops, courses, training programs, mentoring, tutoring, personal development experiences, or any form of education — if your students and clients walk away not just knowing more, but believing more in themselves, this is your award.

You're a great fit if you:

  • Run a business focused on education, training, or personal development

  • Create learning experiences that lead to real, measurable transformation in your students or clients

  • Have a track record of helping people build new skills, confidence, or career pathways

  • Are passionate about making education accessible, engaging, and genuinely life-changing
