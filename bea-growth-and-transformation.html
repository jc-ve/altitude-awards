She Didn't Just Grow a Business. She Transformed It

Growth isn't just about bigger numbers — it's about smarter decisions, braver pivots, and the relentless pursuit of what's next. This award is for the women who looked at their business and said, "We can do better" — and then made it happen.

Whether you scaled from solo to a team, pivoted your entire model, broke into new markets, or completely reinvented what your business looks like — if your growth story has a before and after that makes people's jaws drop, this is your award.

You're a great fit if you:

  • Achieved significant, measurable business growth over a defined period

  • Successfully pivoted, restructured, or reinvented your business model

  • Made strategic decisions that led to transformational results

  • Can demonstrate a clear "before and after" in your business journey
