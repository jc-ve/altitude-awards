She's Not There Yet. But Watch Her Go.

Every success story has a chapter that starts with "I had no idea what I was doing — but I did it anyway." This award is for the women who are right in the middle of that chapter. The ones who've taken the leap, backed themselves, and are figuring it out one brave step at a time.

If you're early in the journey but already proving that you're not someone to underestimate — this is your award.

You're a great fit if you:

  • Are in the first 1–2 years of your business journey

  • Have taken real, tangible steps to turn your idea into a business

  • Show determination, resourcefulness, and a willingness to learn

  • Are building momentum — even if it's one small win at a time
