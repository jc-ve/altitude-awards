Passion & Perseverance Award

Celebrating businesses that demonstrate unwavering passion and resilience — pushing through adversity with commitment and heart.

Enter this Category Download Questions

Built on Grit. Driven by Heart.

Every business has a story — but not every story is easy. This award is for the women who kept going when things got tough. The ones who turned setbacks into comebacks, who built something meaningful through sheer determination and an unwavering belief in what they do.

If your grit has carried you through the hard days and your passion is the reason you're still standing — this is your award.

You're a great fit if you:

  • Built or sustained your business through a challenging period

  • Overcame significant personal or professional obstacles

  • Kept showing up when it would have been easier to walk away

  • Have a story of resilience that inspires others
