Community Builder Award

Celebrating women who bring people together — creating spaces where connection, belonging, and collective growth thrive.

Enter this Category Download Questions

She Didn't Just Find Her People. She Built a Place for All of Them.

Communities don't build themselves — they're built by women who care enough to create the space. This award is for the women who saw a gap, felt the loneliness, noticed the disconnect — and decided to do something about it. The ones who've brought people together around a shared purpose, a common struggle, or a collective dream, and turned that gathering into something powerful.

Whether you've built an online community, a local network, a membership group, a support circle, or a movement that connects people who would never have found each other — if people's lives are better because of the community you created, this is your award.

You're a great fit if you:

  • Founded or lead a community that brings people together around a shared purpose or mission

  • Have created a space where members feel genuinely connected, supported, and valued

  • Can demonstrate the impact your community has had on its members' lives, businesses, or wellbeing

  • Actively nurture, grow, and evolve your community to meet the needs of its members
