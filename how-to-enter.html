How to Enter

Everything you need to submit a strong entry - step by step.

Browse the Categories Enter Awards Today

Entering the Altitude Awards is straightforward - and you don't have to do it all in one sitting. This page walks you through everything, from choosing your category to hitting submit. Take your time, tell your story honestly, and let us take care of the rest.

STEP 1 - Browse the categories and choose yours

Head to the Categories page and read through all the options. Take your time - there's no rush at this stage.

Most entrants choose 1-2 categories that best reflect their story. But you can enter as many as you like. You can enter through a Single, Three-Entry, or Six-Entry Pack, depending on how many categories you'd like to submit.

Not sure which category fits? A good test: which one makes you think "yes, that's me"? Trust that instinct.

Browse the Categories

STEP 2 - Get your assets ready before you start

The entry form will ask for a few things. Having them ready makes the whole process smoother:

  • Your full name and business/organisation name
  • A short bio - 2 to 3 sentences about you and what you do
  • A professional headshot to upload
  • Supporting materials - a website link, media feature, flyer, or anything that helps tell your story

Don't have everything polished? That's okay - share what you have. Judges are looking for your story and your impact, not a perfect media kit.

STEP 3 - Choose Your Pack & Get Started

Choose the entry pack that suits you—Single, Three-Entry, or Six-Entry—and complete your payment to secure your submission.

Once payment is confirmed, you’ll receive an email containing your invoice and your entry link. The same link will also be shown on the thank you page, so you can access it immediately . There are no accounts to create and no software to download.

Entries close midnight 2 August 2026 - start early and come back as many times as you need.

For WWA Members: Your exclusive discount code is included in the payment step. Make sure to use it before checking out.

Enter Altitude Awards Now

STEP 4 - Answer the questions - in your own words

The form guides you through each question one at a time. No trick questions, just answer as honestly and specifically as you can.

A few things that help:

  • Be specific: "I grew revenue by 40% in 12 months" lands harder than "I grew my business"
  • Include community impact: Judges value how your work affects others, not just your own results
  • Don't undersell yourself: If you're unsure whether something counts - include it. Let the judges decide.

All answers are treated with strictest confidentiality. Each category is reviewed by 5 judges who sign confidentiality agreements. Judges with any personal or professional connection to an entrant are excused from that entry.

STEP 5 - Wait for the announcement - and watch your inbox

Our judging panel gets to work after entries close. Finalists announced 7 August 2026 - contacted personally by our team.

Whether a finalist or not, every entrant receives access to our entrant resource pack - judging feedback tools, branded social tiles, and PR templates to help share your story.

Keep an eye on your inbox.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I enter without being nominated?

Absolutely. You can nominate yourself directly - no referral needed. Some women are nominated by a friend or colleague, but self-entry is just as valid and just as welcome.

What if I have a question mid-entry?

Email info@womenwithaltitude.com.au and we'll get back to you as quickly as we can. We're here to help you put your best entry forward.
