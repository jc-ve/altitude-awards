Solo Trailblazer Award

Celebrating the women who are doing it all on their own — building remarkable businesses with nothing but grit, talent, and sheer determination.

Enter this Category Download Questions

No Team. No Safety Net. Just Her.

She's the CEO, the admin, the marketer, the bookkeeper, and the customer service department — all before lunch. This award is for the solo operators who've built something extraordinary without a team behind them. The women who wear every hat, make every decision, and carry every responsibility on their own shoulders.

If you've ever replied to a client email at midnight, filmed your own content, fixed your own website, and still delivered exceptional work the next morning — this award was made for you.

You're a great fit if you:

  • Run your business solo — you are the entire team

  • Have built a sustainable, successful business without employees or partners

  • Demonstrate exceptional resourcefulness, self-discipline, and adaptability

  • Deliver results that prove one person can make a massive impact
