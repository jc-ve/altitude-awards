Inclusivity & Impact Award

Celebrating women who are breaking barriers, championing diversity, and building businesses where everyone belongs.

Enter this Category See Guidelines

She Didn't Wait for the Door to Open. She Built a Bigger Table.

Inclusion isn't a checkbox — it's a commitment. This award is for the women who've made it their mission to ensure that no one gets left behind. The ones who look at their business, their industry, or their community and ask, "Who's missing from this room?" — and then do something about it.

Whether you've created accessible products and services, built a workplace culture where every voice matters, championed underrepresented communities, or designed your business around the belief that everyone deserves to be seen and served — if inclusion is woven into everything you do, this is your award.

You're a great fit if you:

  • Actively champion diversity, equity, and inclusion in your business or industry

  • Have created products, services, or spaces that are accessible to underserved or marginalised communities

  • Built a culture — internally or externally — where people feel seen, heard, and valued

  • Can demonstrate measurable impact in breaking barriers or creating belonging

What Our Judges Want to See

You're Building a World Where Everyone Belongs. Let Us Celebrate That.

The spaces you create, the barriers you break, and the people you champion — that's not just business, that's legacy. Enter the Inclusivity & Impact Award and show the world what real inclusion looks like.
