Social Enterprise/Charity Innovation Award

Celebrating women who are reimagining how we solve social problems — building innovative enterprises and charities that create lasting, systemic change.

Enter this Category See Guidelines

She Didn't Just Start a Charity. She Reinvented How Change Gets Done.

The old way of doing good isn't enough anymore — and some women know it. This award is for the women who've looked at a broken system and thought, "There's a smarter way to fix this." The ones who've built social enterprises or charities that don't just help — they innovate. They find new models, new approaches, and new ways to create impact that's sustainable, scalable, and built to last.

Whether you've developed an innovative funding model, created a self-sustaining social enterprise, launched a charity that operates like a startup, or found a way to turn social impact into a movement — if your approach to doing good is as groundbreaking as the cause you serve, this is your award.

You're a great fit if you:

  • Founded or lead a social enterprise or charity with an innovative approach to creating impact

  • Have developed a model that's sustainable, scalable, or fundamentally different from traditional approaches

  • Can demonstrate measurable social outcomes alongside organisational innovation

  • Are challenging the way the sector operates — proving that doing good and doing it smartly aren't mutually exclusive

What Our Judges Want to See

Last Year's Winner — O.W.N

Older Women's Network (Blue Mountains) - innovative spirit is changing lives, providing security and hope for older women in need. Their work is a powerful example of compassion in action.
