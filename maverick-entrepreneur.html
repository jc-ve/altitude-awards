She Didn't Follow the Rules. She Rewrote Them.

Some women don't fit the mould — because they were never meant to. This award is for the entrepreneurs who zig when everyone else zags. The ones who spotted a gap no one else saw, took the road no one else dared, and built something that made people stop and think, "Why didn't that exist before?"

If you've ever been told your idea was too different, too bold, or too unconventional — and you did it anyway — this is your award.

You're a great fit if you:

  • Built a business that challenges industry norms or conventions

  • Introduced an innovative product, service, or business model

  • Took a creative or unconventional path to success

  • Are known for thinking differently and leading with courage
