The Changemaker (Making a Difference Award)

Celebrating women who see what's broken in the world and have the courage to fix it — creating meaningful, lasting change through their business.

Enter this Category See Guidelines

She Didn't Wait for Someone Else to Fix It. She Became the Solution.

Some women don't just run a business — they run a movement. This award is for the women who looked at a problem in their industry, community, or the world and refused to look away. Instead, they built something that changes things. Something that challenges systems, shifts perspectives, and leaves the world genuinely better than they found it.

Whether you're tackling social injustice, environmental issues, education gaps, health inequities, or any cause that needs a champion — if your business exists because you believe things should be different, this is your award.

You're a great fit if you:

  • Built a business or initiative that directly addresses a social, environmental, or community issue

  • Have created measurable, tangible change — not just awareness, but real impact

  • Challenge the status quo and inspire others to think and act differently

  • Lead with purpose, conviction, and a deep commitment to making things better
