Inclusivity & Impact Award — Social and Community

Celebrating women who are creating a more inclusive world — championing equity, breaking barriers, and building communities where every person matters.

Enter this Category Download Questions

She Sees the People Others Overlook. And She Makes Sure They're Never Invisible Again.

Inclusion at its best isn't a policy — it's a way of living, leading, and loving your community. This award is for the women who've made it their life's work to ensure that no one is left out, left behind, or left unseen. The ones who show up for the people society forgets, who fight for voices that aren't being heard, and who build spaces where everyone — regardless of background, ability, identity, or circumstance — is welcomed and valued.

Whether you work in social services, community development, advocacy, disability support, cultural inclusion, mental health, or any space where equity and belonging are at the heart of what you do — if your impact is measured in lives changed and barriers removed, this is your award.

You're a great fit if you:

  • Lead initiatives, programs, or organisations that promote social inclusion and equity

  • Have created measurable impact in the lives of marginalised, underrepresented, or vulnerable communities

  • Actively break down barriers — systemic, cultural, physical, or social — that prevent people from belonging

  • Lead with empathy, authenticity, and a deep commitment to making your community better for everyone
