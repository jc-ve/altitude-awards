Why Nominate Someone?

Most great work happens quietly. Many of the people who make the biggest difference never put themselves forward.

A nomination is a simple way to change that. It helps us discover individuals and businesses who deserve to be seen, celebrated, and recognised on a larger stage.

Who Can You Nominate?

  • A client or collaborator

  • A business owner or founder

  • A team member or colleague

  • A leader in your industry

  • Someone whose work has inspired you

If they’ve made an impact, they’re worth nominating.

What Happens Next?

Every nomination is carefully reviewed by our team.

Shortlisted individuals are contacted directly and given the opportunity to submit a formal entry. Finalists are then selected and invited to our awards celebration event.

How It Works

Nominating someone is simple:

  1. Share their basic details
  2. Tell us why you’re nominating them
  3. Submit the form

That’s it. No complex steps and no cost involved.

If the person you nominate is shortlisted, we may reach out to invite them to officially enter the awards.
