Honouring Our Women of the Year

We are proud to recognise Kelly Wiseman and Sky Hockley, founders of NeuroTribe, as our Women of the Year and Maverick Entrepreneur Award recipients. Their work is grounded in courage, compassion, and lived experience, and through it they are making a powerful difference in the lives of autistic women and families by creating support that is deeply human, safe, and empowering.
