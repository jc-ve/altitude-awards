Just Getting Started. Already Making Waves.

Some women don't wait for permission to make an impact. They launch, they learn, they adapt — and before anyone expects it, they're already turning heads. This award is for the rising stars. The women in the early stages of their business journey who are already showing the drive, vision, and momentum that set future leaders apart.

If you're new to business but already making things happen — this is your moment to be seen.

You're a great fit if you:

  • Launched your business within the last 1–3 years

  • Are showing strong early growth, traction, or innovation

  • Demonstrate a clear vision for where your business is heading

  • Have the ambition and drive that sets you apart from the crowd
