Program Excellence Award

Celebrating women who design and deliver outstanding programs — creating structured, transformational experiences that get real results.

Enter this Category See Guidelines

She Didn't Just Create a Program. She Built an Experience That Changes People.

Anyone can put together a course outline. But building a program that genuinely transforms the people who go through it — that takes something special. This award is for the women who've poured their expertise, creativity, and heart into designing programs that don't just inform — they deliver real, measurable outcomes that participants carry with them long after it's over.

Whether you've built a group coaching program, a signature course, a corporate training series, a wellness retreat, a mentoring framework, or a community development initiative — if your program is the thing people rave about, refer their friends to, and credit as a turning point, this is your award.

You're a great fit if you:

  • Designed and deliver a structured program that produces consistent, measurable results

  • Have strong participant feedback, testimonials, or completion rates that prove your program works

  • Continuously refine and improve your program based on outcomes and participant experience

  • Create an experience that goes beyond content delivery — your program genuinely transforms participants

What Our Judges Want to See

Your Program Doesn't Just Deliver Content. It Delivers Transformation.

You've built something that changes people — and that kind of excellence deserves to be recognised. Enter the Program Excellence Award and show the world what a truly exceptional program looks like.
