Frequently Asked Questions

What sponsorship opportunities are available?

We offer a range of sponsorship levels — from major naming rights to category sponsorships and in-kind partnerships. Each comes with different levels of brand visibility, event access, and community engagement. Request our sponsorship pack for full details.

How much does sponsorship cost?

Sponsorship packages vary depending on the level of involvement and visibility. We work with sponsors to find the right fit for their budget and goals. Get in touch and we'll walk you through the options.

What do sponsors receive in return?

Depending on your sponsorship level, benefits can include logo placement across all awards promotions, website and social media visibility, gala night branding, speaking opportunities, complimentary tickets, and direct access to our community of professional women.

Who attends the Altitude Awards?

Our community is made up of professional women aged 30–60 across business, leadership, and community — including entrepreneurs, executives, coaches, and changemakers from across NSW and ACT.

Can we sponsor a specific award category?

Yes! Category sponsorship is a great way to align your brand with a specific area — whether that's business innovation, leadership, wellness, or community impact. Your brand becomes the face of that category across all promotions and on gala night.

Can we provide in-kind sponsorship instead of financial?

Absolutely. We welcome in-kind partnerships — whether that's services, products, venues, or expertise. If it adds value to our community or our events, we'd love to hear about it.
