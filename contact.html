Frequently Asked Questions

How do I enter the Altitude Awards?

Simply fill out the registration form, submit your entry, and make your payment — no account needed. You can enter as many categories as you like.

Can I enter more than one category?

Absolutely! There's no limit — enter as many categories as you like across Business, Leadership, and Community.

When do entries close?

Entries close on 30 September. Finalists will be announced ahead of the gala night on 24 October in Sydney.

How much does it cost to enter?

Entry fees vary by category. Register your account to view pricing and purchase entry credits for your chosen categories.

Do I need to attend the gala night?

Finalists are strongly encouraged to attend — it's your moment to celebrate! But attendance is not mandatory.

I'm having trouble with my entry. Who can I contact?

Email us at info@womenwithaltitude.com.au or call 1300 036 615 and we'll help you through it.
