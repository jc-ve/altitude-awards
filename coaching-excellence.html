Coaching Excellence Award

Honouring women who transform lives through exceptional coaching — guiding others to clarity, confidence, and lasting change.

Enter this Category Download Questions

She Doesn't Just Coach. She Transforms.

Behind every breakthrough is a coach who asked the right question at the right time. This award is for the women whose coaching goes beyond frameworks and formulas — the ones who create real, lasting transformation in the lives of their clients. Whether you coach in business, mindset, health, career, or life — if your clients walk away stronger, clearer, and more capable than when they arrived, this award was made for you.

If people's lives genuinely change because of the work you do — it's time the world knows.

You're a great fit if you:

  • Deliver coaching that creates measurable, lasting change in your clients' lives

  • Have a proven methodology, framework, or approach that gets results

  • Consistently receive powerful feedback and testimonials from clients

  • Are committed to your own professional development as a coach
